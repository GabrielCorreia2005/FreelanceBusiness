Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all Chrome extensions published by GabrielCorreiaAI, including:

  • AI Page Summary
  • Focus Tab Dashboard
  • Price Tracker

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data processed by our extensions remains entirely within your browser using Chrome's local storage APIs.

What Data Stays Local

  • AI Page Summary: Page text is analyzed locally. No text is sent to external servers.
  • Focus Tab Dashboard: Tasks, focus items, and settings are stored in chrome.storage.local.
  • Price Tracker: Tracked product URLs and prices are stored locally. Price checks fetch publicly accessible product pages.

Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Permissions

We only request the minimum permissions necessary for each extension to function:

  • activeTab: To read the current page content for summarization or price detection.
  • storage: To save your preferences and data locally.
  • alarms: To schedule periodic price checks (Price Tracker only).
  • notifications: To alert you of price drops (Price Tracker only).

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
