# Chrome Web Store Listing Descriptions

## Extension #1 — AI Page Summary

**Name:** AI Page Summary — Instant Smart Summaries  
**Category:** Productivity  
**Language:** English  

### Short Description (132 chars max)

Instantly summarize any webpage with AI. Get key points, keywords, and reading time in seconds. No login required.

### Full Description

**Stop wasting time reading long articles.**

AI Page Summary gives you the key takeaways from any webpage in seconds — no login, no API key, no sign-up required. Everything runs locally in your browser.

**What you get:**
• **Smart Summary** — Extracts the most important sentences using AI-powered analysis
• **Key Keywords** — See the main topics at a glance
• **Reading Metrics** — Word count, reading time, and compression ratio
• **One-Click Copy** — Copy the summary to clipboard instantly
• **100% Private** — No data leaves your browser

**How it works:**

1. Navigate to any article or page
2. Click the AI Page Summary icon
3. Get your summary in 2 seconds

Built for researchers, students, professionals, and anyone who reads online.

**Privacy:** This extension processes text locally. No data is sent anywhere. No permissions beyond the active tab.

## Extension #3 — Price Tracker

**Name:** Price Tracker — Smart Price Drop Alerts  
**Category:** Shopping  
**Language:** English  

### Short Description (132 chars max)

Track prices on any online store. Set target prices and get notified when prices drop. Works on Amazon, eBay, and more.

### Full Description

**Never miss a price drop again.**

Price Tracker monitors product prices for you and sends browser notifications when the price drops to your target.

**How it works:**

1. Visit any product page (Amazon, eBay, any store)
2. Click the Price Tracker icon — it auto-detects the current price
3. Set your target price
4. Get notified the moment the price drops!

**Features:**
• **Auto Price Detection** — Works on Amazon, eBay, Walmart, and most e-commerce sites
• **Target Price Alerts** — Set the price you want to pay
• **Background Monitoring** — Checks prices even when Chrome isn't in focus
• **Price History** — See how prices have changed
• **Unlimited Products** — Track as many products as you want
• **No Sign-Up** — Works immediately, no account needed

**Supported stores:** Amazon, eBay, Walmart, AliExpress, Best Buy, and virtually any e-commerce site.

**Privacy:** Price data is stored locally. No personal information is collected or shared.
