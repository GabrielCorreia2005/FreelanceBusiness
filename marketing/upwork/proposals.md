## General Upwork Tips

1. **Apply within the first 2 hours** of a job being posted — early applicants get more views
2. **Always ask a specific question** at the end — shows you read the job and increases reply rate significantly
3. **Avoid generic openings** — "Hi, I'm an experienced developer..." → everyone says this
4. **Short proposals win** on Upwork (under 200-250 words beats walls of text)
5. **Replace placeholders** — clients can tell when it's a template, the personalization is key
